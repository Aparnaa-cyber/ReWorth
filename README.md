# 🚀 ReWorth – Premium Imperfect Marketplace

## 📌 Overview

ReWorth is a curated digital marketplace designed to transform unused but slightly imperfect products into valuable opportunities. Instead of discarding items with minor defects, ReWorth enables their redistribution at optimized prices, promoting sustainability and affordability.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

A large number of products are rejected due to:

* Minor scratches
* Packaging damage
* Small cosmetic defects

Despite being fully functional, these products:

* Never reach consumers
* Cause unnecessary waste
* Result in financial losses for businesses

At the same time, many consumers are unable to afford premium products at full price.

---

## 💡 Solution

ReWorth bridges this gap by creating a platform where:

* UNUSED products with minor defects are listed
* Each product is verified and categorized
* Pricing is optimized based on defect severity
* Transparency builds trust with users

---

## 🌍 Impact

ReWorth contributes to:

* ♻️ Waste reduction
* 💰 Affordable access to premium products
* 🏭 Inventory recovery for businesses

Aligned with:
👉 **SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption and Production**

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 🔍 Smart Product Discovery

* Search functionality with real-time filtering
* Category-based product organization

### 🤖 AI-Based Grading System

* Products classified into:

  * Grade A (Minimal defect)
  * Grade B (Moderate defect)
  * Grade C (Visible defect)

### 🛒 Interactive Shopping Experience

* Product grid (50+ items)
* Add to cart functionality
* Buy Now option

### 📄 Defect Verification Certificate

* Each product includes a downloadable certificate
* Contains:

  * Product details
  * Defect description
  * AI grade
  * Pricing comparison
* Displays **VERIFIED** status for trust

### 🎛️ Dynamic Sorting

* Users can filter products based on AI grading

## 🧩 Technology Stack

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. User opens the platform
2. Browses products or searches
3. Applies filters (AI grade)
4. Clicks product to view details
5. Views defect transparency
6. Downloads verification certificate
7. Adds product to cart or proceeds to buy
